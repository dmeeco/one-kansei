We respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain language, what we do (and don't do) with your data.

1. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics scripts. We do not follow you around the web.

2. Minimal Data

If you contact us, we only use your email to reply. We do not share, sell, or use your data for anything else.

3. No Third Parties

Your data stays with us. We do not give it to anyone else.

4. Your Rights

You can ask us to see, correct, or delete your data at any time. Just email info@kansei.com.

5. EU GDPR

We follow the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your rights and privacy are protected.

That's it. No jargon. No tricks. Just respect for your privacy.
